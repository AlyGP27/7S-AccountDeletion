Account Deletion Request

7S Survey System

If you'd like your account and associated data removed from 7S Survey System, you can submit a request using the steps below — whether or not you still have the app installed.

Step 1

Send a deletion request

Email your request to the address below. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request", and include the email address or username registered to your account.

alessandra.polot@bisu.edu.ph

Step 2

What happens to your data

Once your request is verified, the following will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Survey responses and submissions linked to your account
  • Any personal information stored in connection with your account

Step 3

Processing time

Response time

Within 7 business days

Confirmation

Sent via email

7S Survey System — for questions about this process, contact alessandra.polot@bisu.edu.ph
